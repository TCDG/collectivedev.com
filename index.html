About Us

What we do, how we do it, and why we enjoy doing it.


The Collective Developer Group was founded in 2016 as part of an initiative to develop open-source applications for the Discord server Scammer Sub Lounge. Ever since then, TCDG has been expanding and innovating in new ways, and has become what it was today. TCDG is not a full-time (or part-time) job for any of it's members, and we recieve no financial initiatives to develop open-source software. We have a long way to go, and this is just the tip of the iceberg.
