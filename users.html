Our Members

Each and every single one of the TCDG members are amazing and unique developers!


In order for someone to join TCDG they must complete an application forum that is available on our forums. They must provide proof of development, this usually means them just showing us their GitHub or some private projects. This is to ensure that the user meets the development standards that are required. We have a zero tolerance policy for drama in our discord server, if a member is seen to cause drama, they're kicked out.
